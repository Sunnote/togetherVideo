About Together Video

Together video was born because of the need of "cut, copy, paste", and edit open source videos. Logged-in users can edit video stories (generated by the systems) just like Wikipedia's users edit text articles. A story is a continuous sequence of video clips connected following a given structure to convey a story.

The Together Video Team worked on a video auto-editing software and found out that a decent video story often requires a final human touch to be something valuable.

The small team work long hours, around the clock daily to make sure you will have the fully functional service soon. You can now download and enjoy the dual-stream camera app, which is one of the core components of the systems.

Dual-stream Camera App

Dual-stream Camera creates two video outputs in a single capture, so you can shoot more videos with little device storage space left. Just simply touch on screen while shooting to highlight and save the abstract version to your device, the full version will be automatically uploaded to a cloud storage for uninterrupted video capturing experience .
